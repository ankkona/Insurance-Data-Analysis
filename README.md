# 📊 PRISM INSURANCE PVT. LTD. | Insurance Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Insurance Analytics Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze key business metrics related to insurance policies, customers, premiums, and claims. The dashboard transforms raw insurance data into meaningful visual insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor business performance and support data-driven decision-making.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Power BI Desktop**
* **Power Query (ETL)**
* **Data Modeling**

# 🔍 Key Insights

### 1. Travel Insurance Drives Premium Revenue

Travel Insurance contributes the highest premium amount (2.5M), making it the company's strongest revenue-generating policy type. Health and Auto Insurance follow, while Home Insurance contributes the least.

**Business Impact:**
The company can prioritize Travel Insurance through targeted marketing campaigns and customer retention strategies to maximize premium revenue.

### 2. High Percentage of Inactive Policies

Approximately **74.73%** of policies are inactive, while only **25.27%** remain active.

**Business Impact:**
This indicates a significant opportunity to improve policy renewals, customer engagement, and retention programs.

### 3. Claim Rejections Are Higher Than Settlements

Rejected claims are noticeably higher than settled claims, with pending claims representing the smallest portion.

**Business Impact:**
The organization should investigate the reasons behind claim rejections, such as incomplete documentation or policy exclusions, to improve customer satisfaction.

### 4. Adults Generate the Highest Claim Amount

Adults account for approximately **8.8M** in claims, considerably higher than Elder (6.4M) and Young Adult (1.7M) customers.

**Business Impact:**
Adults represent the highest-risk customer segment and should be a primary focus for underwriting, pricing, and risk management.

### 5. Large Coverage Compared to Claims

The company provides a total coverage amount of **600.55M**, while recorded claim payouts amount to only **16.91M**.

**Business Impact:**
This indicates that only a small percentage of the insured coverage has been claimed, suggesting a relatively healthy claim ratio.

### 6. Balanced Customer Demographics

The customer base consists of:

* Female: **5,001**
* Male: **5,003**

**Business Impact:**
The nearly equal gender distribution indicates balanced customer acquisition without significant demographic bias.

### 7. Policy-Level Claim Comparison

The matrix visual allows comparison of Pending, Rejected, and Settled claim amounts across different policy types.

**Business Impact:**
This enables quick identification of policy categories with higher claim liabilities and supports better operational monitoring.
