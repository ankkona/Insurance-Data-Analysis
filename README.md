# 📊 PRISM INSURANCE PVT. LTD. | Insurance Analytics Dashboard

## ► Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Insurance Analytics Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze key business metrics related to insurance policies, customers, premiums, and claims. The dashboard transforms raw insurance data into meaningful visual insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor business performance and support data-driven decision-making.

## ► Tech Stack

* **Power BI Desktop**
* **Power Query (ETL)**
* **Interactive Visualizations**

# ► Key Insights

- Travel Insurance contributes the highest premium amount (2.5M), making it the company's strongest revenue-generating policy type. Health and Auto Insurance follow, while Home Insurance contributes the least.The company can prioritize Travel Insurance through targeted marketing campaigns and customer retention strategies to maximize premium revenue.
- Approximately **74.73%** of policies are inactive, while only **25.27%** remain active.This indicates a significant opportunity to improve policy renewals, customer engagement, and retention programs.
- Claim Rejections Are Higher Than Settlements. Rejected claims are noticeably higher than settled claims, with pending claims representing the smallest portion. The organization should investigate the reasons behind claim rejections, such as incomplete documentation or policy exclusions, to improve customer satisfaction.
- Adults had the highest claim amount **8.8M**, much higher than Elders and Young Adults. This makes adults the key customer group to focus on for managing insurance risk.
- The company provides a total coverage amount of **600.55M**, while recorded claim payouts amount to only **16.91M**. This indicates that only a small percentage of the insured coverage has been claimed, suggesting a relatively healthy claim ratio.
